What is Four Card Blind?

Pick 4 cards you think will win against other decks!

Four Card Blind is a format in Magic the Gathering where players submit a decklist based on only using four cards. Once your deck is approved by the site admins you are then placed in a round robin style tournament against 8-16 players. Each deck is reviewed by an official Magic the Gathering judge to determine the winning outcome, which usually takes about 2-3 days to evaluate.

The standard rules of magic are used, except players do not lose the game for the first card drawn during a player's draw step when there are zero cards in that player's deck. Once the round has been fully resolved, the deck with the most points is victorious, earning its submitter hearty congratulations and each nonbasic card it used a ban in all future rounds.

For a full listing of rules in four card blind, visit our rules page.

What is Four Card Blind?

Four Card Blind is a format in Magic the Gathering where players submit a decklist based on only using four cards. Once your deck is approved by the site admins you are then placed in a bracket style tournament against 8-16 players. Each deck is reviewed by an official Magic the Gathering judge to determine the winning outcome, which usually takes about 2-3 days to evaluate.
